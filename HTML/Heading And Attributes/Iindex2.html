Fantasy Books

Fantasy
Set in magical worlds with wizards, dragons, and mythical creatures. Example: The Lord of the Rings by J.R.R. Tolkien.
Science Fiction
Focuses on futuristic technology, space, and advanced science. Example: Dune by Frank Herbert.
Dark Fantasy
Blends magic with horror, showing the darker side of fantasy worlds. Example: A Song of Ice and Fire by George R.R. Martin.

Adventure Books

Action Adventure
Fast-paced stories full of danger, battles, and physical challenges. Example: Treasure Island by Robert Louis Stevenson.
Exploration Adventure
Focuses on journeys to unknown lands, jungles, or hidden places. Example: Journey to the Center of the Earth by Jules Verne.
Survival Adventure
Centers on characters struggling to survive in harsh or wild environments. Example: The Call of the Wild by Jack London.
